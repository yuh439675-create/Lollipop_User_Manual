Short Drama App User Guide (User Side)

This tutorial is for: Regular users (viewers who watch short dramas, purchase memberships, or invite friends)

System Name: ReelChain Short Drama Platform

Applicable Platforms: iOS, Android App

1. What is a Short Drama App?

Short Drama App It's a mobile app specifically designed for watching short video series. Just like watching TV dramas, each episode is only a few minutes long, making it perfect for watching during short breaks.

Main Features:

  • 🎬 Watch Short Dramas: A vast library of short dramas, watch anytime, anywhere.

  • 💎 Membership Service: Purchase a membership to watch all short dramas for free.

  • 🪙 Coin Top-up: Unlock individual episodes of short dramas with coins.

  • 💰 Earn Money by Inviting: Earn commissions by inviting friends to register.

  • ⭐ Collect and Follow Dramas: Collect your favorite short dramas for easy access later.

2. How to Register and Log In

2.1 Download the App

First, you need to download the short drama app to your phone:

  • Apple Phones: Search for the app name in the App Store and download it.

  • Android Phones: Install via the provided download link.

2.2 Register an Account

The app supports multiple registration methods; choose the most convenient one:

Method 1: Register with Mobile Number (Recommended)

  1. Open the app and click the "Login/Register" button.

  2. Enter your mobile phone number.

  3. Click "Get Verification Code," and the system will send a verification code to your mobile phone via SMS.

  4. Enter the received verification code.

  5. Set a login password (for future logins).

  6. Click "Register" to complete.

Method Two: Email Registration

  1. Select "Email Registration"

  2. Enter your email address.

  3. Click "Get Verification Code" and check your email for the verification code.

  4. Enter the verification code and set a password.

  5. Click "Register" to complete.

Method Three: Third-Party Account Login (Fastest)

Supports the following methods for quick login:

  • Apple Account Login (Apple ID)

  • Google Account Login (Google)

  • Facebook Account Login

  • WeChat Account Login

💡 Tip: After logging in with a third-party account, the system will require you to bind your mobile phone number for enhanced security.

2.3 Enter Invitation Code (Optional) If a friend sent you an invitation code:

  • During registration, find the "Invitation Code" input box

  • Enter your friend's invitation code

  • This way, your friend can receive a referral reward

2.4 Login Account After successful registration, when you open the app in the future:

  • Enter your phone number/email + password to log in

  • Or directly use a third-party account for one-click login

3. How to Watch Short Dramas

3.1 Browse Short Dramas

After opening the app, you will see 4 main tabs (bottom navigation bar):

🏠 Homepage

  • Recommended Short Dramas: Recommended by the system based on your preferences

  • Banner Ads: Displaying popular short dramas

  • Category Filtering: Find short dramas by genre (romance, suspense, urban, etc.)

  • Popular Ranking: The most popular short dramas

📺 Recommendations

  • Video streaming mode, watch short dramas like you're scrolling through TikTok

  • Swipe up and down to switch between different short drama clips

⭐ Following Dramas

  • All the short dramas you're watching are here

  • Easily find the episode you last watched

👤 My Profile

  • Personal information, membership status, wallet balance

  • Favorited dramas, viewing history

  • Invite friends, settings, etc.

3.2 Steps to Watch Short Dramas

Step 1: Select a Short Drama

  1. Find your favorite short drama on the homepage

  2. Click on the short drama cover image

  3. Enter the short drama details page

Step 2: View Details

On the details page, you can see:

  • Short Drama Introduction: Plot summary

  • Episode List: All episodes, such as episodes 1-100

  • Comments Section: Comments from other users

  • Favorite/Share Buttons

Step 3: Select an Episode to Watch

  • Click on a specific episode, such as "Episode 1"

  • Enter the playback page to start watching

3.3 Free Viewing vs. Paid Viewing

Free Viewing:

  • The first few episodes of each short series are free (usually the first 3-5 episodes).

  • You can watch the free episodes first, and decide whether to pay if you like them.

Unlock by Watching Ads:

  • Some episodes can be unlocked for free by watching ads.

  • After watching a 15-30 second ad, the episode can be watched for free.

Paid Viewing: Later episodes require payment, and there are two ways:

  1. Purchase a Membership: Members can watch all short series for free (recommended!)

  2. Unlock with Coins: Purchase a single episode.

4. How to Purchase a Membership

4.1 Benefits of Membership

  • ✅ Watch all short series unlimited times

  • ✅ No ads

  • ✅ Priority viewing of new series

  • ✅ High-definition quality

4.2 Steps to Purchase a Membership

Step 1: Access the Membership Page

  1. Click the "My" tab at the bottom.

  2. Click the "Activate Membership" button below your profile picture.

  3. Or, while watching short dramas, click "VIP Membership - Watch for Free".

Step 2: Choose a Package

The system offers several packages:

  • Monthly Membership: 30 days, e.g., $9.9

  • Quarterly Membership: 90 days, e.g., $19.9 (best deal)

  • Annual Membership: 365 days, e.g., $59.9

💡 Tip: Longer memberships are more cost-effective; quarterly or annual memberships are recommended.

Step 3: Choose a Payment Method

Supports multiple payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)

  • PayPal

  • Stripe Payment

  • Apple In-App Purchase (Apple users)

  • Google Pay (Android users)

  • Nexa Wallet (cryptocurrency payment)

Step 4: Complete Payment

  1. Select Payment Method

  2. Enter Payment Information

  3. Confirm Payment

  4. Membership will be activated immediately after successful payment

4.3 Check Membership Status

  • Go to the "My" page

  • You can see the membership expiration date

  • The system will remind you to renew before expiration

5. How to Top Up Coins

If you don't want to buy a membership, you can top up coins to unlock individual episodes of short dramas.

5.1 Uses of Gold Coins

  • 🪙 1 Gold Coin = 1 Episode of a Short Drama (Price varies depending on the short drama)

  • You can use gold coins to watch only a specific short drama without purchasing a membership.

5.2 Steps to Top Up Gold Coins

Step 1: Access the Top-Up Page

  1. Click the "My" tab

  2. Click "Wallet" or "Gold Coin Balance"

  3. Click the "Top Up" button

Step 2: Choose a Top-Up Package

The system offers several gold coin packages:

  • 💰 50 Gold Coins = $4.9

  • 💰 100 Gold Coins = $9.9

  • 💰 300 Gold Coins = $19.9

  • 💰 500 Gold Coins = $29.9 (Bonus Gold Coins)

💡 Tip: The higher the amount, the more bonus gold coins you receive.

Step 3: Payment

  • Choose payment method (same as membership purchase)

  • Complete payment

  • Gold coins will be credited immediately

5.3 Watching with Gold Coins

  1. Watch the episode that requires payment

  2. System prompt "Requires XX gold coins to unlock"

  3. Click "Unlock with Gold Coins"

  4. Gold coins will be automatically deducted, and the episode will be successfully unlocked

6. How to Invite Friends to Earn Money

This is a share to earn money feature. Invite friends to register and recharge, and you will receive a commission.

6.1 Invitation Reward Rules

Level 1 Invitation (Directly Invited Friends)

  • Friend's successful registration: Reward X yuan

  • Friend's first recharge: You receive X% of the recharge amount

  • Subsequent recharges by the friend: You continue to receive X% commission

Level 2 Invitation (Friends Invited by Friends)

  • Your friend then invites others

  • You also receive a level 2 commission (lower percentage)

📌 Specific commission rates Please check the "Invitation Center" in the app. Different promotions may apply at different times.

6.2 Invitation Steps

Step 1: Obtain Invitation Code/Link

  1. Go to the "My" page
  2. Click "Invite Friends" or "Share to Earn"

  3. The system will generate your exclusive:

  4. Invitation Code: e.g., ABC123

  5. Invitation Link: A website address

  6. Invitation Poster: An image with a QR code

Step 2: Share with Friends

Multiple sharing methods:

  • Copy Invitation Link: Send to a friend, who clicks to register.

  • Share Poster: Save the image and send it to a friend; they can scan the QR code to register.

  • Copy Invitation Code: Tell a friend to fill it in during registration.

Step 3: Friend Registration

  • Your friend downloads the app through your link.

  • Fill in your invitation code during registration.

  • Or fill it in under "Settings - Bind Inviter" after registration.

Step 4: Receive Rewards

  • You receive a registration reward when your friend successfully registers.

  • You receive a commission when your friend makes a recharge.

  • Commissions are automatically credited to your "Wallet Balance".

6.3 View Invitation Earnings

  1. Go to "My" → "Invitation Center".

  2. You can see:

  3. Total Number of Invitations

  4. Today's Earnings / Total Earnings

  5. Invitation Leaderboard (See who earns the most)

  6. Click "Earnings Details" to view the source of each commission.

7. How to Withdraw Earnings

The money you earn by inviting friends can be withdrawn to your own account.

7.1 Withdrawal Requirements

  • ✅ Account balance ≥ Minimum withdrawal amount (e.g., $10)

  • ✅ Linked withdrawal account (Alipay, bank card, PayPal, etc.)

7.2 Withdrawal Steps

Step 1: Linking a Withdrawal Account

For first-time withdrawals, you need to link your account first:

  1. Go to "My" → "Wallet"

  2. Click "Withdrawal Account Management"

  3. Select withdrawal method:

  4. Alipay: Enter your Alipay account and real name

  5. Bank Card: Enter your bank card number, issuing bank, and cardholder name

  6. PayPal: Enter your PayPal email address

  7. Nexa Wallet: Enter your wallet address

  8. Submit for review (generally approved in 1-3 days)

Step 2: Apply for Withdrawal

  1. Go to the "Wallet" page

  2. Click the "Withdraw" button

  3. Enter the withdrawal amount (e.g., $50)

  4. Select the withdrawal account

  5. Confirm Submission

Step 3: Wait for Funds to Arrive

  • System Review (1-3 business days)

  • After approval, the money will be transferred to your linked account.

  • You can check the progress in "Withdrawal Records"

7.3 Withdrawal Precautions

  • ⚠️ Daily withdrawal limit (e.g., a maximum of 2 times per day)

  • ⚠️ Withdrawal amount must be an integer (e.g., $10, $20)

  • ⚠️ Some payment methods may incur transaction fees.

  • ⚠️ Ensure your withdrawal account information is accurate, otherwise the funds will not arrive.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What should I do if I don't receive the verification code during registration?

A:

  • Check if your phone number is entered correctly.

  • Check if your SMS has been blocked (see the "Spam" folder).

  • Wait 1-2 minutes; the verification code may be delayed.

  • Try registering with your email address.

  • Contact customer service for assistance.

Q2: Do I still need coins after purchasing a membership?

A:

  • No! Members can watch all short dramas for free.

  • Choose either coins or membership.

  • Recommendation: Membership is more cost-effective for frequent short drama viewers.

Q3: How do I continue watching a short drama if I'm halfway through?

A:

  • The system automatically records your viewing progress.

  • The next time you open the short drama, it will resume playback from where you left off.

  • Or go to the "Following Dramas" page and find the short drama.

Q4: How do I save my favorite short dramas? A:

  1. Go to the short drama details page.

  2. Click the "Favorite" button (⭐ icon).

  3. You can view your favorite short dramas in "My → Favorites".

Q5: What should I do if a short drama won't play/plays failed?

A:

  • Check your network connection (Wi-Fi is recommended).

  • Switch the resolution (click on player settings).

  • Restart the app and try again.

  • Update the app to the latest version.

  • Contact customer service for feedback.

Q6: My invited friend hasn't bound my invitation code?

A:

  • Your friend needs to fill in the invitation code during registration.

  • If you forgot to fill it in during registration:

  • Go to "My → Settings".

  • Find "Bind Inviter".

  • Enter your invitation code.

  • Submit the binding.

Q7: How do I contact customer service? A:

  1. Go to the "My" page.

  2. Click "Help Center" or "Online Customer Service".

  3. You can view frequently asked questions or ask questions online.

  4. You can also contact customer service via Telegram (the customer service link is in the app settings).
